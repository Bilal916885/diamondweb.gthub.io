The Basics

Price impact is the term used to describe the influence that a trade has on the value of a specific cryptocurrency. Specifically, it measures the degree to which the price of cryptocurrency is altered due to a buy or sell action.

How DiamondSwap works.

Existing holders create their own token pools for investors to purchase. These pools are visible via the DiamondSwap Marketplace. All trades are facilitated as peer-to-peer swaps to avoid price impact and tax (where transfer tax = 0%).

1.Existing holders list tokens for sale

Tokens are listed on the DiamondSwap Marketplace instantly, and transfer to the DiamondSwap Smart Contract.

2. Investors search listings & buy direct from holders

Purchased tokens are deposited into buyers wallet instantly via the DiamondSwap Smart Contract.

3. No price impact!

All trades are facilitated as direct transfers between buyers and sellers, thereby eliminating the potential price impact.
